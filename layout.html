About Us:

Our mission is to provide relief to those affected by floods.
Flood Relief is an award winning organisation, providing disaster response & preparedness services across India.
As a disaster relief agency, we help people to survive and rebuild their lives through food, medical, education, shelter and livelihood programs.
Our most recent interventions in Maharashtra, Karnataka and Assam have impacted thousands of lives!
